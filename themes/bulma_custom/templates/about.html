Space Quest is a space and astronomy themed "scavenger hunt", originally created for the National Astronomy Meeting 2023, held in Cardiff, UK, as part of the public engagement projects. Please see the NAM 2023 website public engagement pages for more information.

The concept and website are maintained by Edward Gomez. The illustrations and posters were created by Alice Hopkinson. The project is supported by Las Cumbres Observatory.

Space Quest is open source and is licensed under the Creative Commons Attributions - Share Alike - Non-commercial license.
