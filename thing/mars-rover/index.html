Mars Rover

Mars is the only planet we know of, that is entirely inhabited by robots!

To explore our Solar System, humans always send machines before people. To send people to Mars would be much more expensive than sending probes and robots. Mars has had many robot visitors in the past and currently have 2 active robot cars or rovers on it. They have tools for investigating the ground and for digging and drilling underneath the surface. They have also onboard mini laboratories for performing experiments.
